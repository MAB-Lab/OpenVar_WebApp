About OpenVar

OpenVar is deep genomic variant annotator. It differs from other annotators as it supports a polycistronic annotation model of human genes. OpenVar is built around the well-known SNPEff annotator, and supports Ensembl, NCBI RefSeq and OpenProt annotations.

Using OpenVar can uncover the pathological potential of previously overlooked genomic variants. Preliminary studies highlight that up to 30% of genomic variant currently ignored because falling in non-coding regions, or because leading to a synonymous mutations, may be pathological as they alter currently non-annotated open reading frames (ORFs).

For more information about unannotated ORFs, please visit: www.openprot.org
